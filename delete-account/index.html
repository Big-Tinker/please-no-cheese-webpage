Delete Account

If you have created an optional account in Please no cheese and wish to permanently delete it, you can request removal by email.

Send a message to kontakt@bigtinker.se asking that your account be deleted. Please include:

  • Your username
  • Your email address, if you registered one

We will process your request within a reasonable time and confirm when your account and associated data have been permanently removed from our servers.

Deleting your account does not automatically clear the random device ID stored on your device; you can remove that separately by clearing the App’s cache in your device settings. See our Privacy Policy for details.
